# Success Criteria & Metrics

## Business Metrics (KPIs)
| Metric | Definition | Target (MVP) |
|--------|------------|--------------|
| **Activation Rate** | % of visitors who generate at least one page. | > 40% |
| **Export Ratio** | % of generated pages that are downloaded. | > 25% |
| **Referral Coefficient** | Users sharing their generated sites. | k > 1.1 |

## Technical SLOs (Service Level Objectives)
| Metric | Definition | Threshold |
|--------|------------|-----------|
| **API Latency** | Time for Gemini to return JSON. | < 8s (p95) |
| **Uptime** | Availability of the application. | 99.9% |
| **Error Rate** | Failed generations due to parsing logic. | < 2% |

## User Experience Goals
- **"Wow" Factor**: User sentiment upon first seeing the preview (measured via qualitative feedback).
- **Editability**: Users should feel the AI got 80% of the way there, requiring minimal refinement.
